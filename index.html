London Underground Facts

Five tube lines (Victoria, Central, Jubilee, Northern and Picadilly) run a 24 hr service on Fridays and and Saturdays.

11 Tube Lines serve Greater London, along with the Docklands Light Railway (DLR) and an interconnected local train network. From Monday to Saturday, underground trains generally run between 5am and midnight. Sunday provides reduced operating hours.

Londoners usually use the Oyster card to pay for all of their public transport needs.
